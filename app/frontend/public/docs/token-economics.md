# Token Economics

## LDAO Token Overview

The LDAO token is the native utility and governance token of the LinkDAO ecosystem.

## Token Distribution

- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 LDAO
- **Community**: 40% (400M)
- **Team**: 20% (200M) - 4-year vesting
- **Treasury**: 20% (200M)
- **Liquidity**: 10% (100M)
- **Advisors**: 5% (50M) - 2-year vesting
- **Ecosystem**: 5% (50M)

## Token Utility

### Governance

- Vote on proposals
- Create proposals
- Delegate voting power

### Staking

- Earn rewards
- Increase voting power
- Access premium features

### Marketplace

- Pay for listings
- Reduced fees
- Priority placement

### Community

- Create communities
- Access gated content
- Earn rewards

## Staking Mechanics

### Staking Tiers

| Tier | Minimum Stake | APY | Benefits |
|------|--------------|-----|----------|
| Bronze | 1,000 LDAO | 5% | Basic rewards |
| Silver | 10,000 LDAO | 10% | + Reduced fees |
| Gold | 50,000 LDAO | 15% | + Priority support |
| Platinum | 100,000 LDAO | 20% | + Governance bonus |

### Lock Periods

- **Flexible**: 0% bonus, withdraw anytime
- **30 days**: 10% APY bonus
- **90 days**: 25% APY bonus
- **180 days**: 50% APY bonus

## Fee Structure

### Marketplace Fees

- **Standard**: 2.5% per transaction
- **LDAO Holders**: 1.5% (>1,000 LDAO)
- **Stakers**: 1.0% (>10,000 LDAO staked)

### Fee Distribution

- **Treasury**: 40%
- **Stakers**: 30%
- **Liquidity**: 20%
- **Burn**: 10%

## Deflationary Mechanisms

### Token Burns

- 10% of all fees burned
- Quarterly buyback and burn
- Governance-approved burns

### Supply Reduction

Target: Reduce supply to 500M over 5 years through burns.

## Liquidity Incentives

### LP Rewards

Provide liquidity on DEXs to earn:
- Trading fees
- LDAO rewards
- Bonus multipliers

## Governance Rights

### Voting Power

1 LDAO = 1 vote (base)

**Multipliers:**
- Staked 30 days: 1.1x
- Staked 90 days: 1.25x
- Staked 180 days: 1.5x

## Token Vesting

### Team Vesting

- 1-year cliff
- 4-year linear vesting
- Monthly unlocks

### Advisor Vesting

- 6-month cliff
- 2-year linear vesting
- Monthly unlocks

## Economic Security

### Price Stability

- Liquidity pools on major DEXs
- Treasury reserves
- Buyback mechanisms

### Anti-Whale Measures

- Maximum transaction limits
- Gradual vesting schedules
- Quadratic voting for governance

## Future Developments

- Cross-chain bridges
- Additional staking tiers
- Enhanced utility features
- Ecosystem expansion
