The LinkDAO marketplace features a tier-based system that rewards sellers for their performance and engagement. As you grow your store and improve your customer service, you'll unlock new benefits and features that can help you succeed on the platform.

## Understanding Tiers

The tier system consists of five levels, each with increasing benefits and requirements:

### 1. Bronze Tier (Entry Level)
**What you get:**
- Create unlimited active listings
- Standard 5% platform commission rate
- Access to basic seller tools
- Weekly withdrawal limit of $1,000

**Requirements:**
- Connect your wallet and create a seller profile
- No minimum sales or rating requirements

**Perfect for:** New sellers just getting started with their online store

### 2. Silver Tier
**What you get:**
- Create unlimited active listings
- Reduced 4.5% platform commission rate
- Access to basic analytics dashboard
- Priority customer support
- Weekly withdrawal limit of $5,000

**Requirements:**
- Complete $5,000 in sales
- Maintain 4.0+ star rating
- Maintain average response time under 1 hour

**Perfect for:** Growing sellers with consistent sales and good customer service

### 3. Gold Tier
**What you get:**
- Create unlimited active listings
- Lower 4% platform commission rate
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Priority customer support
- Access to marketing tools
- Weekly withdrawal limit of $25,000

**Requirements:**
- Complete $25,000 in sales
- Maintain 4.3+ star rating
- Maintain average response time under 30 minutes
- Keep return rate below 5%

**Perfect for:** Established sellers with strong performance metrics

### 4. Platinum Tier
**What you get:**
- Create unlimited active listings
- Lowest 3.5% platform commission rate
- Dedicated account manager
- Early access to new platform features
- Custom store branding options
- Weekly withdrawal limit of $100,000

**Requirements:**
- Complete $100,000 in sales
- Maintain 4.5+ star rating
- Maintain average response time under 15 minutes
- Keep return rate below 3%
- Keep dispute rate below 1%

**Perfect for:** High-performing sellers with exceptional service

### 5. Diamond Tier (Elite Level)
**What you get:**
- Create unlimited active listings
- VIP 3% platform commission rate
- White-glove support service
- Revenue sharing opportunities
- Exclusive seller events
- Advanced API access
- No withdrawal limits
- Custom store branding

**Requirements:**
- Complete $500,000 in sales
- Maintain 4.7+ star rating
- Maintain average response time under 10 minutes
- Keep return rate below 2%
- Keep dispute rate below 0.5%
- Maintain repeat customer rate above 30%

**Perfect for:** Top-performing sellers who exemplify excellence

## How Tier Progression Works

### Automatic Evaluation
Your tier is automatically evaluated based on your performance metrics. The system checks your status regularly and upgrades you when you meet the requirements for a higher tier.

### Progress Tracking
You can track your progress toward the next tier through your seller dashboard. The system will show you:
- Which requirements you've already met
- What you need to achieve for the next tier
- Estimated time to reach the next tier
- Specific actions you can take to improve your standing

### Benefits Activation
When you're upgraded to a new tier, your new benefits are activated immediately. You'll receive a notification and can start using your new features right away.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often is my tier status evaluated?
A: Your tier status is evaluated automatically on a regular basis. When you meet the requirements for a higher tier, you'll be upgraded automatically.

### Q: Can I lose my tier status?
A: Yes, if your performance metrics decline significantly, you may be moved back to a lower tier. However, you can regain your status by improving your metrics again.

### Q: Do I lose benefits if I drop to a lower tier?
A: If your tier status changes to a lower tier, your benefits will adjust to match the new tier. However, you can always work to regain higher tier status.

### Q: Are there any fees to move up tiers?
A: No, tier progression is completely free. You simply need to meet the performance requirements to unlock higher tiers.

### Q: Can I see my current tier and progress?
A: Yes, your current tier and progress toward the next tier are displayed in your seller dashboard with clear visual indicators.

### Q: Do tier benefits apply retroactively?
A: Benefits apply from the moment you reach a new tier. Any new commission rates will apply to future sales, not past sales.
