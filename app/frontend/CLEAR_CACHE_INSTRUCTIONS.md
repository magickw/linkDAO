# Cache Clearing Instructions

To ensure the navigation fix takes effect, please follow these steps to clear your browser cache:

## Chrome/Edge/Brave:

1. Open Developer Tools (F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I / Cmd+Option+I)
2. Go to Application tab
3. In the left sidebar, click "Service Workers"
4. Click "Unregister" next to any service workers listed
5. In the left sidebar, click "Clear storage"
6. Click "Clear site data"
7. Close and reopen the browser

## Firefox:

1. Open Developer Tools (F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I / Cmd+Option+I)
2. Go to Application tab (or Storage tab in newer versions)
3. Click "Service Workers" in the left sidebar
4. Click the "Unregister" button
5. Go to Settings (gear icon) > Privacy & Security
6. Scroll to "Cookies and Site Data"
7. Click "Clear Data..."
8. Check both boxes and click "Clear"

## Safari:

1. Develop menu > Empty Caches (if Develop menu is not visible, enable it in Safari Preferences > Advanced > Show Develop menu)
2. Safari menu > Preferences > Privacy
3. Click "Remove All" under Cookies and website data
4. Restart Safari

## Alternative Method (All browsers):

1. Close all browser windows
2. Open browser in incognito/private mode
3. Navigate to the application

After clearing the cache, restart your browser and try navigating the application again.
